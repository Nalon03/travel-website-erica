Santorini, Greece

Things to See and Do

Visit the caldera views in Oia, explore Akrotiri archaeological site, relax on Red Beach, and enjoy local cuisine.

How to Get There

Fly into Santorini International Airport (JTR) or take a ferry from Athens port (Piraeus), approximately 5–8 hours.

Best Time to Visit

April to October. Peak season is July and August. Spring and autumn offer pleasant weather with fewer crowds.

Kyoto, Japan

Things to See and Do

Visit Fushimi Inari Shrine, Arashiyama Bamboo Grove, Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion), and traditional tea houses.

How to Get There

Fly into Osaka Kansai International Airport (KIX), then take the Haruka Express or Shinkansen to Kyoto Station (75 minutes).

Best Time to Visit

March to May for cherry blossom season, or October to November for autumn foliage.

Cape Town, South Africa

Things to See and Do

Take the cable car up Table Mountain, visit Boulders Beach penguin colony, explore the Cape Winelands, and walk along the V&A Waterfront.

How to Get There

Fly into Cape Town International Airport (CPT), which has direct connections from major European and African cities.

Best Time to Visit

November to March (Southern Hemisphere summer) for warm, dry weather ideal for outdoor activities.
