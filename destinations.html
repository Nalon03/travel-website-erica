Destinations

Hong Kong

Things to See and Do

Visit Victoria Peak for panoramic views of the skyline, explore the lively Temple Street Night Market, take a ride on the historic Star Ferry, and visit the Giant Buddha on Lantau Island.

How to Get There

Fly into Hong Kong International Airport (HKG). Direct flights are available from major cities worldwide. The Airport Express train connects the airport to the city centre in 24 minutes.

Best Time to Visit

October to December offers cool temperatures and low humidity. Spring (March to May) is also a pleasant time to visit before the summer heat and humidity arrives.

Rome, Italy

Things to See and Do

Explore the Colosseum, visit the Vatican and the Sistine Chapel, throw a coin into the Trevi Fountain, and stroll through the historic streets of Trastevere.

How to Get There

Fly into Rome Fiumicino Airport (FCO). The Leonardo Express train connects the airport to Roma Termini station in 32 minutes. Rome is also well connected by rail across Europe.

Best Time to Visit

April to June and September to October offer the most pleasant weather with comfortable temperatures and fewer crowds compared to the busy summer season.

Madrid, Spain

Things to See and Do

Visit the Prado Museum and its world-class art collection, stroll through Retiro Park, explore the Royal Palace, and experience the vibrant nightlife of the Gran Via and La Latina districts.

How to Get There

Fly into Adolfo Suárez Madrid–Barajas Airport (MAD), one of Europe's busiest hubs with direct connections from cities worldwide. Metro Line 8 links the airport to the city centre in around 15 minutes.

Best Time to Visit

Spring (March to May) and autumn (September to November) offer mild temperatures and fewer crowds. Summer can be very hot, though the city stays lively with outdoor festivals throughout the year.

Moscow, Russia

Things to See and Do

Walk through Red Square and marvel at St Basil's Cathedral, explore the Kremlin, visit the world-famous Tretyakov Gallery, and ride the stunning Moscow Metro, which is an attraction in itself.

How to Get There

Fly into Sheremetyevo International Airport (SVO) or Domodedovo Airport (DME). The Aeroexpress train connects Sheremetyevo to Belorussky railway station in about 35 minutes.

Best Time to Visit

Late spring (May to June) and early autumn (September) offer pleasant temperatures and long daylight hours. Winter visits offer a magical snow-covered city, though temperatures can drop well below freezing.

New Zealand

Things to See and Do

Explore the breathtaking fjords of Milford Sound, visit the geothermal wonders of Rotorua, hike the Tongariro Alpine Crossing, and tour the famous Hobbiton movie set in the Waikato region.

How to Get There

Fly into Auckland Airport (AKL) for the North Island or Christchurch Airport (CHC) for the South Island. Both airports have direct and connecting flights from major cities in Asia, Australia and beyond.

Best Time to Visit

December to February (New Zealand summer) is ideal for outdoor activities and beach trips. March to May offers beautiful autumn colours with fewer tourists and comfortable temperatures across both islands.

Macau

Things to See and Do

Visit the Ruins of St Paul's, explore the colourful Senado Square, discover the historic Taipa Village, and experience the dazzling Cotai Strip with its world-class entertainment and casino resorts.

How to Get There

Reach Macau by ferry from Hong Kong (about an hour) or fly into Macau International Airport (MFM), which has connections across Asia. The city is compact and easy to explore on foot or by free casino shuttle buses.

Best Time to Visit

October to December is the best time to visit, with cooler and drier weather. Spring (March to May) is also pleasant. Avoid July and August when typhoon season and high humidity can affect travel.
