Destinations

Hong Kong

Things to See and Do

Visit Victoria Peak for panoramic views of the skyline, explore the lively Temple Street Night Market, take a ride on the historic Star Ferry, and visit the Giant Buddha on Lantau Island.

How to Get There

Fly into Hong Kong International Airport (HKG). Direct flights are available from major cities worldwide. The Airport Express train connects the airport to the city centre in 24 minutes.

Best Time to Visit

October to December offers cool temperatures and low humidity. Spring (March to May) is also a pleasant time to visit before the summer heat and humidity arrives.

Rome, Italy

Things to See and Do

Explore the Colosseum, visit the Vatican and the Sistine Chapel, throw a coin into the Trevi Fountain, and stroll through the historic streets of Trastevere.

How to Get There

Fly into Rome Fiumicino Airport (FCO). The Leonardo Express train connects the airport to Roma Termini station in 32 minutes. Rome is also well connected by rail across Europe.

Best Time to Visit

April to June and September to October offer the most pleasant weather with comfortable temperatures and fewer crowds compared to the busy summer season.
