5 Days in Santorini: A Complete Guide

15 March 2024

Santorini is everything you imagine and more. From the iconic blue-domed churches of Oia to the volcanic beaches, every corner of this island is breathtaking. Here's how I spent my five days and my top tips for making the most of your visit.

Travel Tips

  • Book accommodation in Oia or Fira for the best caldera views.
  • Hire an ATV to explore the island independently.
  • Watch the sunset from Oia Castle — arrive early to get a good spot.
  • Try local dishes like fava, tomatokeftedes, and fresh seafood.

Cherry Blossom Season in Kyoto

2 April 2024

There is nothing quite like seeing Kyoto in full bloom. The cherry blossom season transforms the city into a pink paradise. Maruyama Park, Philosopher's Path, and the grounds of Kiyomizu-dera are absolute highlights during this magical time of year.

Travel Tips

  • Book accommodation months in advance — cherry blossom season fills up fast.
  • Visit temples early in the morning to avoid crowds.
  • Get a week-long bus pass for easy travel around the city.
  • Try a traditional kaiseki meal for an authentic Kyoto experience.

My First Time in Cape Town

20 January 2024

Cape Town completely blew me away. The combination of stunning mountain scenery, beautiful beaches, vibrant culture, and incredible food makes it one of the most diverse destinations I have ever visited. Table Mountain alone is worth the trip.

Travel Tips

  • Check weather conditions before booking the Table Mountain cable car.
  • Visit the penguins at Boulders Beach — it's unforgettable.
  • Explore the Cape Winelands for a day trip.
  • The V&A Waterfront has great restaurants and markets.
